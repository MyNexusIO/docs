### Find a Campaign (First Time)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Login to Nexus" icon="link">
    Ensure that you are logged into your account by visiting app.mynexus.io and using your account credentials.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click &#x22;Find a Campaign&#x22;">
    ![](/findcampaign.jpeg)

    <Note>
      If you do not see the "Find a Campaign" option, it may mean you are already added to a campaign. Please see the section below titled "Find a Campaign (Multiple)"
    </Note>


  </Step>

  <Step title="Input Organization Code">
    Each campaign is assigned a 4-digit code that they can provide to volunteers. Once the code is inputted, the system will automatically check for a campaign that matches.

    ![](/campaigncode.jpeg)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click &#x22;Confirm&#x22;">
    If a campaign is found with that code, you will be shown a green checkmark with the campaign name. To finish adding yourself to the campaign, simply click "Confirm".

    ![](/campaignsuccess.jpeg)

    <Warning>
      If the system is not finding the intended campaign, we recommend reaching out to a campaign administrator to verify the code. <u>Nexus will not provide organization codes to anyone other than campaign administrators.</u>
    </Warning>


  </Step>
</Steps>

### Find a Campaign (Multiple)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Login to Nexus">
    Ensure that you are logged into your account by visiting app.mynexus.io and using your account credentials.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click on Arrow in Top Right Corner">
    ![](/volunteer-dashboard.jpg)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click &#x22;Settings&#x22;">
    After clicking the arrow next, you will notice a dropdown menu appear. Simply click "Settings".
  </Step>

  <Step title="Navigate to the &#x22;Organization&#x22; Tab">
    There will be tabs listed near the top of the popup. Locate the "Organization" tab and click on it.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click the Plus Button">
    You will see an icon with a plus button next to the header. Click that to prompt the organization code input.

    ![](/addorganization.jpg)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Input Organization Code">
    Each campaign is assigned a 4-digit code that they can provide to volunteers. Once the code is inputted, the system will automatically check for a campaign that matches.

    ![](/orgcodeinput.jpg)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Confirm">
    If a campaign is found with that code, you will be shown a green checkmark with the campaign name. To finish adding yourself to the campaign, simply click "Confirm".

    ![](/campaignsuccess2.jpeg)
  </Step>
</Steps>
