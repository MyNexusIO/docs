### Account Creation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Nexus" icon="link">
    Visit the Nexus homepage by going to app.mynexus.io in your browser of choice (web or mobile)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click &#x22;Create a New Account&#x22;">

  </Step>

  <Step title="Input Account Information">
    To create your account, you will need to provide your first name, last name, email, and a password.

    <Tip>
      **Safety Tip:** As an added measure of security, Nexus will require that your password meets certain criteria. Your password must be 8 characters long and include an uppercase character, special character, and a number. **Do not share this password with anyone.**
    </Tip>


  </Step>

  <Step title="Click &#x22;Sign Up&#x22;">
    The last step to creating your account is clicking the Sign Up button. You will be redirected to your dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>
